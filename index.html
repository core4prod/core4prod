Dylan Irving

Director, Shooter & Editor

Dylan Irving is a professional and recent graduate from Durham College's Video Production program. With over 4 years dedicated to honing his skills and mastering the art of video creation, Dylan is a great addition to any team. Dylan's expertise spans a wide spectrum within the world of video production. Proficient in Camera operation and Cinematography, he brings a keen eye for detail and composition to every project. His passion for storytelling is evident in his roles as a Director and Writer, where he excels in crafting compelling narratives that resonate with audiences. His proficiency in Video Editing, using the Adobe Creative Suite, ensures that his visions are realized with precision and finesse. As Dylan Irving continues to make his mark in the world of video production, his passion, creativity, and work ethic serve as a testament to his potential for continued success and innovation in the industry.

Jerry Marshall

Lead Editor, Shooter, VFX & Developer

Jerry Marshall is a professional videographer who has been shooting, editing, and producing videos for over a decade. Since becoming a professional videographer in 2020, he has produced content for dozens of companies, organizations, and events across Southern Ontario, including Ontario Tech University, Market Logics, Duntroon Highlands, and South Georgian Bay Tourism. He has also worked closely with H Visual Media to produce videos for Honda, Blue Mountain Resort, One World Music Festival, the Great Northern Exhibition, and more.

Max Carter

Shooter, Editor & Head of social media management

Max Carter is a versatile and skilled videographer with over five years of experience in the industry. With a passion for visual storytelling and a talent for capturing life's moments, Max has honed his craft to deliver exceptional results for his clients. Specializing in collaboration and professionalism, Max works closely with his clients to bring their vision to life. Whether it's a promotional video, real estate video, or a corporate event, Max ensures that every project is completed with the highest level of quality and attention to detail. Max's skill set extends beyond traditional videography. As an experienced drone operator, he is able to capture stunning aerial footage that adds a unique perspective to his videos. Additionally, Max is proficient in video editing, framing, and cinematography, ensuring that every frame is perfect and every moment is captured beautifully.
